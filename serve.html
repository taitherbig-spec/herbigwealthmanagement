Home/Who We Serve

Built for people at
every stage of wealth.

Herbig Wealth Management works with individuals, families, business owners, and nonprofits across Houston and beyond — from those just starting to build wealth to those managing complex, multi-generational financial situations.

01
Corporate
Executives
Houston's Corporate Sector

High-income executives face a unique set of financial challenges: concentrated equity positions, complex compensation structures, deferred income, and the need to convert earnings into lasting wealth efficiently. We specialize in the intersection of compensation planning, tax strategy, and long-term wealth management.

  • RSU & Stock Option Planning
  • Deferred Compensation Strategy
  • Concentrated Position Management
  • Executive Benefits Optimization
  • Tax-Efficient Wealth Accumulation
02
Business Owners &
Entrepreneurs
Small Business · Mid-Market

Business owners often have most of their net worth tied up in their company — and no clear plan for what comes next. We help owners separate personal wealth from business risk, design tax-efficient retirement plans for their team, and plan for eventual transitions whether that means a sale, succession, or transfer to family.

  • Business Succession Planning
  • SEP-IRA & Solo 401(k) Strategy
  • Entity Structure & Tax Planning
  • Key-Person Risk Management
  • Exit & Liquidity Planning
03
High-Net-Worth
Individuals
$1M+ Investable Assets

Clients with significant investable assets need more than portfolio management — they need a fully integrated financial strategy that minimizes taxes, manages complexity, and positions wealth to last across generations. We deliver that coordination with the personal attention of an independent advisor, not the scale of a wirehouse.

  • Direct Indexing & TLH
  • Estate & Legacy Planning
  • Charitable Giving Strategy
  • Multi-Account Tax Coordination
  • Alternative Asset Evaluation
04
Retirees &
Affluent Families
Wealth Preservation · Income

Retirement brings a new set of financial challenges: sustainable income, healthcare costs, RMD management, and the desire to leave something meaningful behind. We help retirees and their families navigate the distribution phase with confidence — spending intentionally without fear of running out.

  • Retirement Income Strategy
  • RMD Planning & Roth Conversions
  • Social Security Optimization
  • Medicare & Long-Term Care
  • Multi-Generational Wealth Transfer
05
Nonprofits &
Foundations
Mission-Driven Organizations

Nonprofits and foundations deserve the same quality of financial stewardship as individual clients. We help organizations invest their assets responsibly, design retirement plans for their staff, and advise boards on fiduciary responsibilities — so leadership can stay focused on mission.

  • Endowment Investment Policy
  • 403(b) Plan Design & Oversight
  • Board Fiduciary Education
  • Charitable Giving Coordination
  • Financial Wellness for Staff
06
Emerging
Wealth
Building From a Strong Foundation

You don't need to be wealthy to benefit from great financial advice — in fact, the earlier you start, the more it matters. We work with young professionals and growing families who want to build smart habits, maximize their savings, and make confident financial decisions from the start.

  • Financial Plan Foundation
  • Student Loan Strategy
  • First Home & Major Purchase Planning
  • 401(k) & IRA Optimization
  • Insurance & Protection Review
Specialized Focus

Houston Energy Industry
Professionals

Houston is the energy capital of the world — and its workforce has some of the most complex financial situations in any industry. Pension options, deferred compensation, equity grants, and concentrated positions in employer stock require specialized expertise. Herbig Wealth Management has deep familiarity with the compensation structures of the region's major energy employers.

XOM
ExxonMobil
Pension coordination, RSU planning, savings plan optimization, and concentrated XOM position management for current and former employees.
COP
ConocoPhillips
Benefits analysis, equity compensation strategy, and retirement planning for one of Houston's largest employers.
CVX / SHEL
Chevron & Shell
Deferred compensation, defined benefit coordination, and wealth management for energy sector professionals navigating complex benefit packages.
ET / LNG
Midstream & LNG
Portfolio strategy and income planning for professionals and investors with significant midstream energy exposure.
HAL / SLB
Oilfield Services
Financial planning for Halliburton, SLB, and oilfield services professionals navigating cyclical income and equity compensation.
P66 / VLO
Refining & Downstream
Comprehensive wealth management for refining industry professionals at Phillips 66, Valero, and related companies.
