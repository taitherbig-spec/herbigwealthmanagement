01 · Financial Planning

A plan as dynamic as your life.

True financial planning is not a document you file away — it's a living strategy that evolves as your career, family, and goals change. At Herbig Wealth Management, we build comprehensive plans that connect every financial decision you make into a cohesive, forward-looking picture.

We start with a deep discovery process: where you are today, where you want to go, and what's standing in the way. From there, we build a roadmap covering cash flow, debt, savings rates, protection, and long-term wealth accumulation — all stress-tested against real scenarios.

Whether you're building from scratch or optimizing an existing financial picture, you'll leave every planning meeting with clarity, confidence, and specific next actions.

02 · Investment Management

Portfolios built on discipline, not noise.

Most investors underperform not because they pick the wrong stocks, but because they make emotional decisions when markets get uncomfortable. Herbig Wealth Management builds disciplined, evidence-based portfolios tailored to your risk tolerance, tax situation, and time horizon — and we stay the course with you through volatility.

For clients with substantial non-retirement assets, we offer direct indexing — owning individual securities instead of ETFs — which enables precise tax-loss harvesting and customization that a fund simply can't provide. This approach is particularly powerful for taxable accounts above $250K.

Every portfolio is constructed with an eye toward after-tax, after-fee returns. We don't chase performance; we build systems that compound quietly over time.

03 · Tax Strategy & Preparation

Tax planning that happens before April.

Most people experience tax planning as something that happens to them in April. We flip that: tax strategy is a year-round discipline that shapes every investment, retirement, and income decision you make.

Herbig Wealth Management integrates tax thinking into every aspect of your financial plan — from Roth conversion strategies to capital gain harvesting windows, from qualified dividend optimization to the timing of stock option exercises. We work alongside your CPA to implement strategies, not just identify them.

For clients with complex situations — equity compensation, rental income, business ownership, or significant capital events — proactive tax strategy can be the highest-ROI work we do together.

  • CPA coordination & tax return review

04 · Retirement Planning

Retirement built around your life, not a calculator.

Retirement planning is more than projecting a savings rate and picking a number. It's about understanding what you want your life to look like — and building a financial structure that makes it sustainable through market cycles, inflation, healthcare costs, and longevity risk.

We analyze your full retirement picture: 401(k) contribution strategy, IRA and Roth optimization, Social Security timing, pension coordination (if applicable), and income distribution sequencing. For clients with employer retirement plans, we also review plan design, fund selection, and fiduciary exposure.

The goal isn't just to retire — it's to retire with confidence and never run out of money.

  • IRA & Roth IRA strategy

Protect what you've built before the unexpected happens.

08 · Estate Planning & Wealth Transfer

Build a legacy that reflects your values.

Estate planning isn't just for the ultra-wealthy — anyone with assets, dependents, or specific wishes for the future needs a plan. Without one, the state decides how your estate is distributed, often at significant cost and delay to your family.

Herbig Wealth Management coordinates the financial dimensions of estate planning alongside your estate attorney: beneficiary designations, asset titling, trust funding, charitable giving strategies, and the tax-efficient transfer of wealth to the next generation. We also help clients and their families think through the values and intentions behind a legacy — not just the mechanics.

For business owners and high-net-worth families, we engage with advanced strategies including GRATs, SLATs, IDGTs, and family limited partnerships to maximize what transfers to heirs and charitable causes.
