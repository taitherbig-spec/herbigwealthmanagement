About Tait Herbig

Advice driven by experience, not commission.

After graduating from Texas Tech University with a degree in Finance, Tait built his expertise at Equitable Advisors and Capitalwise before founding Herbig Wealth Management in 2026. His approach is rooted in a simple belief passed down by his family: understand the business, understand the market, and always put the client first.

Based in Houston, Tait works with clients across the full spectrum of wealth management — from retirement plan optimization to direct indexing and tax-loss harvesting strategies. He brings the same precision and care to your portfolio that he brings to everything he does.
